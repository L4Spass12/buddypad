### Escape on your desk

The **BuddyPad Adventure** collection starts from a simple observation: our desks are often where we spend most of our waking time, and they badly lack visual openness. An Adventure pad is a permanent window onto a landscape that lets the space breathe.

### The flagship moods

#### Mountain landscapes

Ridges at sunset, snowy peaks, foggy valleys - inspires calm and a sense of perspective.

#### Forest scenes

Misty undergrowth, autumn forests, lone trees - ideal for a desk in a cozy environment (remote work, studio).

#### Oceans and coasts

Waves, cliffs, sunsets over water - brings a sense of space and horizon.

#### Deserts and mesas

American landscapes, ochre reds, "road trip" aesthetic - more graphic, perfect for a setup with a vintage or creative vibe.

### How to fit a landscape into your setup

The trick: **pick a landscape whose dominant tones match the rest of the desk**. A sunset valley (orange/purple) pairs with warm ambient lighting. A Nordic forest (cool green/blue) works better with neutral light and a light-wood desk.

### Size and visual impact

XXL 90x40 cm is **the ideal size** for this collection: panoramas take their full dimension. In standard size, go for more centered designs (a lone tree, a lake, an isolated mountain) to keep visual punch.
