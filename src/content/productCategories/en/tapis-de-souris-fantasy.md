### Imagination at mouse-reach

A **fantasy pad** turns a desk into an extension of the universe you love. Whether you play JRPGs, write fiction, or simply love imaginary worlds - aesthetics matter as much as technical performance.

### The major fantasy styles

#### Classic medieval-fantasy

Dragons, castles, knights, enchanted forests. The universe popularized by Tolkien, D&D, Warhammer.

#### Dark fantasy

Dark moods, mysterious creatures, eerie landscapes. Influences from Bloodborne, Dark Souls, Berserk.

#### Colorful heroic fantasy

Brighter landscapes, vivid magic, heroic characters. More visually accessible.

#### Mythology and legends

Samurai, vikings, mythological figures - crossover with the manga category for Japanese designs.

### Play with ambient lighting

A fantasy pad reaches its full impact in a curated ambiance:

- **Purple/blue LEDs** to reinforce the mystical atmosphere
- **Dimmable desk lamp** to create different moods at different hours
- **Monitor with low-light mode** for evening sessions
- **Figurines or decor objects** that extend the universe of the pad

### For long gaming sessions

MMO and JRPG players often spend 3-4 hours in a row in front of the screen. A well-chosen fantasy pad is part of the experience - it strengthens immersion without distracting from gameplay, especially in XXL where the eye always catches a bit of the background landscape.
