### Girly, a fully owned aesthetic

A **girly mouse pad** isn't a compromise: it's a strong aesthetic choice, with the same technical quality bar as every other category. Dusty pink, soft pastels, floral or marbled motifs - a girly desk is often more visually harmonious than an overloaded gaming setup.

### The shades that work best

#### Pastel pink and dusty pink

The safe bets, work with everything: light wood, white, chrome, celadon.

#### Pink and gold marble

More luxurious, ideal for a creative work desk (design, content, photo).

#### Terracotta and salmon pink

The 2025-2026 trend, warmer than classic pink, very flattering on a desk.

### Pairing a girly pad with the rest of your gear

A pink pad pairs beautifully with:

- A white or pink keyboard (MX Blue or Brown switches now come in pastel)
- A white, pink or silver mouse
- A light-wood monitor stand
- A soft pink LED lamp (not aggressive RGB)

### Durability and maintenance

Pink is a color that **shows dirt marks more easily** than dark tones. Clean more often (every 1-2 months) with lukewarm water and mild soap. Strictly avoid bleach, which yellows pink fibers.
