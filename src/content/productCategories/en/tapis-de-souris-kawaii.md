### Kawaii aesthetics, way more than a trend

**Kawaii** (可愛い, "cute" in Japanese) is a full-on visual culture: soft colors, lovable characters, comforting motifs. On a PC setup, a kawaii pad adds a touch of personality that breaks the uniformity of classic gaming gear - all black, all RGB, all aggressive.

### The most popular kawaii styles

#### Pastel and pastel goth

Pink, lavender, mint, sky blue tones. Soft vibe, perfect for a desk in a bedroom or a studio.

#### Cute animals

Sleeping cats, bunnies, pandas, plush bears. The bestsellers of the category.

#### Stylized Japanese culture

Cherry blossoms, sakura, cute-version geishas, traditional motifs with a sweet twist.

### Choosing between dense or minimal pattern

A **dense pattern** (illustrated landscape, full scene) is more visually impactful but can be distracting if you also run design software next to it. A **minimal pattern** (small elements on a solid background) is more versatile and works with every setup.

### Maintenance to preserve the print

Kawaii pads use sublimation printing that soaks into the fibers - so far more durable than surface printing. A gentle sponge clean every 2-3 months is enough to keep the colors looking new.
