### Manga and anime at the heart of your setup

A **manga** or **anime mouse pad** turns a plain desk into a piece of self-expression. Whether you're into classic shonen (battles, epics), darker seinen, or the colorful shojo aesthetic, the pad is the perfect canvas to show off your world without taking over the whole room.

### The main design families

#### Samurai and traditional Japanese culture

Katanas, armor, cherry blossoms, mountain landscapes. Often a darker aesthetic, accented with red or gold.

#### Contemporary anime

Stylized characters, action scenes, hero portraits. Heavily graphic designs with sharp lines.

#### Kawaii manga

Crossover with the kawaii category: cute characters in classic manga style (big eyes, exaggerated expressions, pastel palette).

### Print quality and durability

All our manga pads use **thermal sublimation printing**: pigments soak into the fabric rather than sitting on its surface. Result: vibrant colors, UV resistance, and zero degradation when washing. A well-cared-for anime pad easily holds its punch for 3-5 years.

### Recommended format by design

**Full scenes** (landscapes, panoramas) shine in XXL 90x40 cm. **Portraits or icons** stay punchy in the standard format. Up to you depending on the space your desk gives you.
