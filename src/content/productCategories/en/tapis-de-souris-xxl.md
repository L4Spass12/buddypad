### Why XXL changes everything

An **XXL mouse pad** isn't just for the Instagram aesthetic. It solves three concrete problems: the edge of a classic pad cutting your flick mid-action, scratches on a wooden desk, and a keyboard that slides on a surface different from the mouse. On a 90x40 cm format or larger, everything becomes uniform.

### How to pick the right XXL

#### Sizes in detail

- **90x40 cm (35x16 in)**: standard, ideal for a 120-140 cm desk
- **100x50 cm (39x20 in)**: premium comfort, 160 cm+ desk
- **120x60 cm (47x24 in)**: full deskmat, for a 180 cm desk

#### The fabric

**Tightly woven cloth** is still the benchmark: balanced glide, pleasant feel, sleek or printed look. **Faux-leather** desk mats look more premium but are slightly less mouse-friendly - go for them if your desk is mostly office work.

#### The finish

A solid XXL pad has stitched edges (no fraying) and a **non-slip rubber base without spikes** (won't mark the wood underneath).

### XXL and competitive gaming

FPS pros almost all play on XXL because they use very low DPI (400-800). A wide arm sweep equals a full screen rotation, which demands proportional space for the mouse. On a standard pad, the surface ends mid-flick: guaranteed miss.
