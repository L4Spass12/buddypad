### The ultimate combo: RGB + wireless charging

**RGB LED pads with wireless charging** represent the absolute top tier in gaming accessories. You never have to think about charging your mouse again - it stays charged continuously while you play.

### How does it work?

The pad has a built-in **Qi charging coil** (the standard wireless charging spec) in a dedicated zone. When a compatible mouse enters that zone, charging activates automatically. No cable, no separate dock - the mouse charges in its natural environment.

### The real day-to-day benefits

- **Never a dead mouse** in the middle of a match
- **No more cable on the desk** (the wireless mouse really becomes wireless)
- **Visually cleaner setup**
- **Compatible with mice that have no visible battery** (ultra-lightweight)

### Limits to know

Not every mouse is compatible. Only mice designed with a Qi receiving coil can charge this way. Before buying the pad, absolutely verify that **your current mouse is compatible** - otherwise you'll need to upgrade both.

### Brands and ecosystems

#### Logitech PowerPlay

Logitech's proprietary system, works with G Pro Superlight, G502 Lightspeed and other compatible G mice. Very reliable.

#### Razer Hyperflux

Razer's system, more limited in compatibility but perfectly integrated with the Razer Chroma ecosystem.

#### Qi standard

"Open" pads compatible with any Qi mouse - more flexible but sometimes less optimized in charging speed.

### RGB comfort as a bonus

On top of wireless charging, these premium pads include full customizable RGB backlighting, often synced with your keyboard and mouse via the brand's software. The setup becomes a real cohesive product, not just an assembly of accessories.
